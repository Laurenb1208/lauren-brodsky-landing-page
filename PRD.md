# Product Requirement Document

## 01 — Problem Statement

Lauren Brodsky is a Business Analytics & Information Systems student at the University of Iowa, graduating in May 2026. The goal of this landing page is to provide a simple professional website that introduces Lauren’s background, skills, and experience.

The page allows hiring managers and professional connections to quickly understand Lauren’s academic focus, technical skills, and professional interests. Unlike a resume, the landing page provides a central online location where visitors can learn about projects, experience, and contact information.

---

## 02 — Target Audience

The primary audience for this landing page includes:

- Hiring managers at nonprofit organizations that use data to improve impact
- Hiring managers or recruiters at technology companies
- Professional connections and networking contacts

Visitors to the page are looking to quickly understand:

- Educational background
- Technical skills
- Relevant experience
- Professional interests
- How to contact Lauren

---

## 03 — Goals & Success Metrics

| Goal | How Success Is Measured |
|-----|-----|
| Increase professional visibility | Visitors access the landing page |
| Create networking opportunities | Networking contacts reach out |
| Encourage engagement | At least 5 visitors click LinkedIn or GitHub links |
