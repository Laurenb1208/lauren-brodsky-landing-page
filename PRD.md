# Product Requirement Document

## 01 — Problem Statement
Lauren Brodsky is a Business Analytics & Information Systems student at the University of Iowa, graduating in May 2026. She is interested in data-focused roles in nonprofit and technology organizations.

This landing page gives hiring managers and professional connections a central place to learn about Lauren’s background, skills, and experience beyond what a resume alone can show.

## 02 — Target Audience
The main audience for this landing page is hiring managers at nonprofit organizations using data for impact, along with professional connections interested in Lauren’s work and background.

These visitors want to quickly understand her projects, skills, experience, and how to contact her.

## 03 — Goals & Success Metrics

| Goal | How We Measure It |
|---|---|
| Increase professional visibility | Visitors view the landing page |
| Create networking opportunities | Networking connections reach out |
| Encourage engagement | 5+ people click LinkedIn or GitHub |
